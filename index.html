Step 1: Setting Up Zoom Meeting (Enabling Registrants)

  • Log in to the Zoom Account using host@roaimastermind.com.
  • Create a Zoom meeting.
  • Ensure that Registration is enabled.
  • Copy the Meeting ID.
  • Go to Rainmaker Workflows.
  • Search for the Project Grandline folder.
  • Inside the folder, open the Add Registrant to Zoom workflow.
  • Go to Step 3 of the workflow and edit the URL that contains the Meeting ID:
    https://api.zoom.us/v2/meetings/{{meetingID}}/registrants
  • Click Save on the Step 3 Action.
  • Click Save in the upper right corner of the workflow.

Step 2: Adding Mastermind Members to Zoom Meeting

  • Go to the Rainmaker Contacts section.
  • Select the contacts you want to add to the Mastermind Zoom session.
  • Add a tag like mastermind or any tag you pre-set in the Add Registrant to Zoom workflow.
  • They will then be added to Zoom as registrants automatically.

Step 3: Joining the Mastermind Session

  • After completing Steps 1 and 2, users will be able to join the mastermind session via sso.peterswain.com or using the redirect link found in their contact information in Rainmaker.

How to Join Mastermind Session as a Member

  • Go to sso.peterswain.com.
  • Log in using your mastermind email and the default passcode, letmein.
  • Upon successful login, you will automatically be admitted to the mastermind session and redirected to the user dashboard.
  • If not redirected after 5 seconds, click the Join button on the user dashboard.

How to Access the Member Engagement Dashboard

  • Go to sso.peterswain.com/admin.
  • Enter your Admin Credentials.
  • Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the Member Engagement Dashboard.
  • The dashboard will display engagement statuses for mastermind members as Green, Amber, Red, and Black.

How to Deactivate a Member's Subscription and Remove Them from Zoom as Registrants

  • Go to the Rainmaker Contacts section.
  • Select the contacts you wish to remove from the mastermind Zoom session.
  • Remove the tag, such as mastermind, or any tag you pre-set in the Add Registrant to Zoom workflow.
  • The contacts will be automatically removed from Zoom as registrants.

How to Renew a Mastermind Subscription

  • Go to https://ageoftheartist.com/signmeup.
  • Enter the member's information.
  • Enter payment details.
  • After successful registration, the user will be able to log in via sso.peterswain.com.
